About Me

Austin Sapin, 20, is a sophomore at the University of Miami, majoring in Journalism and Sports Administration. Sapin is from Washington D.C. and graduated from Bethesda Chevy-Chase High School. Sapin has been writing for CaneInsider, a website covering Miami Hurricanes baseball and basketball for the past two years. As a writer, he has attended events such as the 2014 ACC Baseball Tournament and the 2015 NIT Semifinals and Finals at Madison Square Garden. As a high school student he attended a summer program for sports journalism at American University. Sapin also was the main sports writer on his school newspaper, The Tattler and occasionally acted as editor of the sports section.
